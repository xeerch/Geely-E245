## Release Notes
Hello!<br>
We're bringing you a new OTA (Over-the-Ait) update to make your driving experience even better. Here's what's new in this update:<br>

1. Addition of Android Auto.<br>
2. The Acoustic Vehicule Alerting System (AVAS) provides three sound source options.<br>
3. Addition of one-tap user shortcut button for quick settings.<br>
4. Addition of support for trunk open/close/pause functionality on the negative home screen.<br>
5. New audible reminder for drive mode switching.<br>
6. General optimization of other in-vehicule infotainment system functions.<br>
   
We hope you enjoy the enhanced features and thank you for choosing GEELY.<br>
